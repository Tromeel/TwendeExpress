About Us

We are a Kenyan-based transport company dedicated to providing fast, safe, and reliable transport solutions across the country and the East African region. From local deliveries and long-distance cargo to passenger and executive transport, we connect people and businesses with efficient, affordable service. With a strong focus on professionalism and timely delivery, we’re your trusted partner on the road.

  • Good Connectivity

    Our locations with reliable internet and communication infrastructure in order to support digital booking and tracking systems

  • Access to Major Transport Corridors

    Located near major highways such as the Nairobi-Mombasa Highway, Northern Corridor, and Western Corridor for seamless nationwide and cross-border operations

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking

    Enables customers and company staff to monitor the exact location of vehicles and shipments in real time via web or mobile apps.

Our Services

We provide fast and reliable transport for goods and passengers across Kenya. Our services include cargo haulage, last-mile delivery, passenger shuttles, and cross-border transport.

Storage

At Twende Express, we offer secure and affordable storage solutions to meet your short-term and long-term warehousing needs. Our facilities across Kenya are designed to safely store goods of all types—whether you're holding stock before delivery, waiting on distribution, or managing seasonal inventory. With 24/7 security, inventory tracking, and easy access to transport services, we make storage simple, safe, and reliable for businesses of all sizes.

Logistics

At Twende Express, we provide comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to the unique needs of Kenyan businesses. From route planning and cargo handling to timely deliveries and real-time tracking, we ensure your goods move efficiently and safely across Kenya and the East African region. Our experienced team coordinates every step of the supply chain to simplify your operations and help your business grow.

Cargo

At Twende Express, we specialize in reliable and efficient cargo transport across Kenya and East Africa. Whether you need to move agricultural products, industrial goods, or retail merchandise, our modern fleet and experienced drivers ensure your cargo arrives safely and on time. We offer flexible options for both small and bulk shipments, backed by real-time tracking and excellent customer support.

Trucking

At Twende Express, we offer dependable trucking services across Kenya, delivering goods quickly and safely to your destination. Our fleet of well-maintained trucks handles everything from bulk cargo to specialized shipments, serving industries like agriculture, construction, and retail. With professional drivers and real-time tracking, we ensure your freight moves efficiently, no matter the distance.

Packaging

At Twende Express, we provide professional packaging solutions to ensure your goods are secure and protected during transit. Our skilled team uses quality materials tailored to your product’s needs, whether fragile, bulky, or perishable. By combining careful packaging with reliable transport, we help deliver your items safely across Kenya and beyond.

Warehousing

At Twende Express, we offer secure and flexible warehousing solutions to help businesses store their goods safely and efficiently. Our facilities are equipped with modern security systems and easy access for quick loading and dispatch. Whether you need short-term storage or long-term inventory management, we support your supply chain needs across Kenya.

Frequently Asked Questions by you

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be changed or cancelled up to 2 hours before departure. Cancellation may attract a small fee depending on the time and route.

What types of transport services do you offer?

We provide cargo transport, passenger services, courier deliveries, and logistics solutions across Kenya and neighboring countries.

How do I book a transport service?

You can book via our website, mobile app, customer service hotline, or directly at our offices.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, cash payments, and mobile money platforms like Airtel Money.

Do you provide real-time tracking of shipments?

Yes, for cargo shipments, we offer GPS tracking to monitor your goods during transit.
